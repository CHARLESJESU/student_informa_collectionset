ADMISSIONS

OVERVIEW OF ADMISSION

Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology offers 09 Undergraduate, 01 Postgraduate programmes and Research Centers on 03 departments. All the undergraduate and postgraduate programmes are offered in engineering and technology disciplines and research centers are availed for engineering, technology and in Science and humanities. Research Centers, integral of respective department encourage inter-departmental collaborative participation of students in exciting research projects. A student admitted should register in one of the research centers depending on the degree/ programme selected and their own interest to pursue

PROGRAMMES OFFERED

Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology offers the under graduation degrees in Bachelor of Engineering(B.E.) and Bachelor of Technology(B.Tech.) on various disciplines, and the Postgraduation degree in Master of Engineering(M.E.) and Research Centers in the departments of engineering, technology, basic science and humanities. All the undergraduate and postgraduate programmes are approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. Also all the programmes which are offered on various disciplines, have been accredited by NBA (National Board of Accreditation).

RESEARCH CENTERS

Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology offers well-equipped laboratories and qualified supervisors for doing Ph.D. research as a scholar on a full or part-time basis. The research centers are approved by Anna University, Chennai. Our institution encourages research in various disciplines by funding stipends to full-time research scholars and providing various facilities and leave with salary for successful completion of research activities for part-time scholars. Also, the faculties are enormously guiding and supporting students to trigger and stimulate their inventions and innovations in research through participation in conferences, publication of articles in technical journals, completion of mini-projects, and real-time projects.
